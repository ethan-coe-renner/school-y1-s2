Shannon's expansion method for creating 4:1 mux, b and c selects

f = b'c'(a'd'0+a'd0+ad'0+ad0)

+b'c(a'd'0+a'd0+ad'1+ad1)

+bc'(a'd'1+a'd0+ad'1+ad0)

+bc(a'd'1+a'd1+ad'1+ad1)
